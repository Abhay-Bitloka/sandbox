Competitions

To enhance literary skills and powers of expression, the following competitions are conducted. A few are :

Literary

Debate, Recitation, Essay writing, Picture description, Cross-word puzzle, etc.

Cultural

To keep up our culture and tradition, literature and art-form, the following competitions are conducted every year. Group Song, Group Dance, Dramatics, Solo song and dance, Rangoli, Cookery, Mehandi designing to name a few.

Competitive Exams

To help students think differently, improve their application skills and prepare them for advanced learning they are motivated to take up competitive exams conducted by various National and International Organisations viz.

  • All India General Knowledge Exam
  • Math and Science Olympiad
  • Diagnostic test conducted by ASSET (Educational Initiatives)
  • IA&AS
