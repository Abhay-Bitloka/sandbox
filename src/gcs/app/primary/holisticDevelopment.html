World Literary Day / Reading Day

Conducted on the last Friday of November every year.

  • “One Nation Reading together.”
  • “Words are the root of everything. When we read books, we begin to exist.”
  • “If there were no words, how could we even think?”

World Environment Day

Celebrated on 5th June every year. Students prepare charts, slogans and write-ups on the topic.

Focus on our culture

To imbibe our culture, Bhagavadgita, Vishnusahasranama, Bhajagovindam, Hanuman Chalisa, Bhajans and Shlokas are recited by our students.

To inculcate noble values in students we have Spiritual Education Programme wherein resource persons are invited to address the students on value based education.

Language Development Programme

To inculcate reading habit and enhance language ability Newspapers (Student edition) from Times of India subscribed for students. A period per class is allotted every week. A resource person from NIE (News paper in Education) conducts various activities to improve linguistic skills in students.
