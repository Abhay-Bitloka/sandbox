Activities

Rhyme and Rhythm

Delightful and enjoyable rhyme sessions get the children involved in singing by which their phonological awareness is enhanced.

Story time

Story session includes the use of attractive teaching aids like flash cards, puppets, stuffed toys with voice modulation and dramatization from the teachers which help in excellent understanding leading to recapitulation by the children.

Concept time

Early learning skills are focused by asking thought provoking questions and introducing new vocabulary during general knowledge time.
